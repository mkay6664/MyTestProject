About

The New York Property Group LLC (“NYPG”) is a commercial real estate firm headquartered in New York City. We have produced this zine to showcase how tastes and preferences are changing in the City. Our clients consist of real estate developers, owners and operators. We hope that the sites assists our clients in finding the best use of retail space designed for serving food to NYC consumers.
