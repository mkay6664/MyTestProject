Food Festivals

By Matt Kay
December 21, 2018, 8:00PM EST

Outdoor food festivals are growing in popularity. Whether you are in the mood for BBQ, vegan, or a liquid diet, there is probably a delicious New York area food festival happening near you.

Photo by Robert Bye

New York | Spring is just around the corner and the New York food festival scene is starting to come alive.

Ritual is hosting a $1 food festival in Midtown for a full week in March. Ritual is a popular app that allows uses to order ahead to skip the line at the brick and mortar location.

The company has partnered with more than 200 local NYC vendors to produce the “Midtown Food Fest.” You can buy a ticket for $1, and you will be able to purchase a meal typically priced at $10. The festival begins March 14 and ends March 22.’

Columbia Wine Company is hosting a Saint Patrick’s Day celebration on Saturday, March 16 at 4038 Broadway. The event will feature a variety of whiskey, liquor and treats from Ireland.

US Veg Corp is hosting its ninth annual event that showcases plant-based food and products. “NY Vegetarian Food Festival” will take place between May 18 and May 19 at the Metropolitan Pavilion. The event will feature chef demos, speakers, vendors, activities for kids, wellness activities and more.
